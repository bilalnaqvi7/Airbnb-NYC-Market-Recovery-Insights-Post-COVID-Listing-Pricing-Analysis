# Airbnb NYC Market Recovery Insights (Post-COVID Listing & Pricing Analysis)

## Overview
This project analyzes Airbnb NYC listings to support business recovery planning post-COVID by identifying high-demand boroughs, customer booking patterns, and pricing ranges that drive maximum traction.

## Business Objective
- Understand customer preferences and booking behavior across NYC
- Identify price bands and room types that drive higher traction
- Provide recommendations for inventory acquisition and pricing strategy

- Highest listing concentration in **Manhattan (44%)** and **Brooklyn (41%)**
- Strongest booking volume observed in **1â€“6 minimum nights**
- Clear long-stay demand spike at **30 days** (also visible at 60 & 90)
- Most preferred customer price band: **$40â€“$190**
- Customers prefer **Entire home/apt** and **Private rooms** over shared rooms

## Recommendations
- Expand listings in the **$40â€“$190** price range for higher demand capture
- Target acquisitions:
  - **Private rooms** in Manhattan & Brooklyn
  - **Entire homes** in Bronx & Queens
- Increase monthly rental supply (30/60/90-day stay windows)
- Promote shared rooms with targeted discounts to unlock additional bookings
